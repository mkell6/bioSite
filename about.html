About Andrew Lee

Andrew Lee is a 24 year old Texas native who's found himself living in Nebraska for his job. He's part of a large family, being one of ten siblings. Andrew shares his life with his wife Marissa, their energetic dog Sammy, and Shelly, their aging but adorable cat. They have all been living in Nebraska for the past few years. Looking ahead to the future, Andrew has set his sights on completing his cybersecurity program at Bellevue University. He is set to graduate this summer, which he could not be more ready to do. His ambition is to launch a career in the tech industry, ideally back in Texas where his roots are.
